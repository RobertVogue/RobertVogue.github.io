Intro

While part of the Human Resources team, I’ve built both interpersonal and organizational skills but I missed the creative freedom that was inherent in development. In order to grasp my new-found love from programming, I have worked hard to improve my skills in JavaScript, Python, Nodejs, React, and more.

As a League of Legends patron for 8 years, I have learned so much about mechanics, teamwork, and the ability to “stay cool.” The overall design of the game is not overwhelming and easy to use. Respect the framework which gives the players complete control over their experience.

I am a contributor who loves to learn and share knowledge. I enjoy helping others understand techniques I have used in development. This sharing of knowledge is powerful and efficient, I thrive in a collaborative environment and actively seek opportunities to contribute. For this reason and along with my skills, I know I will make a great addition to the team.
